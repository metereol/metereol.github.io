WHAT?

Metereol (pronounced material) is an experimental typographic weather visualizer. Built with P5.js, Metereol uses real-time weather data to create an animated typographic expression of how the weather feels outside.

WHO?

Stephen Kistner is an identity & motion graphics designer interested in the intersection of design and digitally generative processes. Metereol was developed as coursework under the direction of Kiel Mutschelknaus.
